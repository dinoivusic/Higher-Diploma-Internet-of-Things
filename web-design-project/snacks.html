My brazilian inspired party snacks are a must in every party, with diferent shapes, sizes and flavours it can fulfill everyones savoury needs, from the famous coxinha to the perfect prawn tartlet.

Our Snacks

Salmon Crostini

Thin slices of toasted bread, brushed with olive oil topped with smoked salmon. Elegant and tasty!

Bruschetta

Thick slices of bread grilled, drizzled with olive oil, and topped with tomatoes and cream cheese. Delicious appetizer!

Potato Rounds

Baked potato rounds topped with cream cheease and bacon. A great and tasty combination!

Mini Quiche

Small tart shells filled with cheese and bacon creating a hearty mini quiche for brunches or dinner parties.

Coxinha

Popular Brazilian snack made of chopped chicken meat covered in dough, molded into a shape of a chicken leg battered and fried.

Cheese Balls

Very popular snack in Brazil consisting of a cheese spread in the shape of a ball.
